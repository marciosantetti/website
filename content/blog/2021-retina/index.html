Motivation

Several times I got frustrated with blurry plots in Python.

I thought: I have a high-resolution display, so why to my plots do not look beautiful?

Here’s the solution

After some digging, I found the easiest solution. In your Jupyter notebook, type the following code (I recommend doing this in the same chunk where you load your modules, e.g., numpy, pandas, matplotlib):

%config InlineBackend.figure_format = 'retina'

… and it couldn’t be easier!

Happy (high-resolution) plotting!
